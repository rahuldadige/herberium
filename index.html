Neem tree 3D model

Scientific name: Azadirachta Indica

Uses :
1. Skin care - Used to treat acne, eczema, and other skin conditions
2. Dental care - Neem twigs are traditionally used as toothbrushes to maintain oral hygiene
3. Medicinal - Neem leaves and bark are used to treat infections, fever, and digestive issues.

Places

Neem trees are primarily found in South Asia, particularly in India, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka, but they also grow in tropical and subtropical regions worldwide.

Conditions

Neem trees thrive in hot, arid to semi-arid climates with temperatures ranging from 21°C to 32°C (70°F to 90°F). They prefer well-drained sandy or loamy soils but can grow in a variety of soil types.

Remedies

1. Consuming neem juice or supplements is believed to purify the blood and boost immunity.
2. Neem oil is used to treat dandruff, lice, and promote healthy hair growth.
